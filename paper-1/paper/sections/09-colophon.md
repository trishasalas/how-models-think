## Colophon

This paper is set in Atkinson Hyperlegible, designed by the Braille Institute to maximize legibility for readers with low vision through distinctive letterforms and numerals that reduce character misrecognition. Choosing it for a paper about accessibility is not incidental.
The PDF was produced using Pandoc with LuaLaTeX. A custom template.tex enables \textbackslash{}DocumentMetadata tagging, producing a document that conforms to both PDF/UA-2 (universal accessibility) and PDF/A-4f (archival) standards. This ensures compatibility with screen readers and other assistive technologies.
This paper was written in collaboration with Claude (Anthropic). The research, methodology, analysis, and conclusions are the author's own.
