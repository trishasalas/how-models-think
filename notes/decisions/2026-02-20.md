# Decisions — 2026-02-20

## Restructure repo as three-paper monorepo

**Context:** how-models-think was organized as a flat repo with notebooks/ and
results/ at the root alongside src/ and notes/. Planning to publish as a
series of three papers and needed a structure that reflects that.

**Decision:** Reorganize into paper-1-emergence/, paper-2-probing/,
paper-3-mechanistic/ directories. Existing notebooks and results moved to
paper-3-mechanistic/. Shared src/ and notes/ stay at root. README updated
with series overview.

**Why monorepo over separate repos:**
- src/ library is shared across all three papers
- DECISIONS and OBSERVATIONS cover the full research arc, not individual papers
- Single repo URL simplifies citation and discovery
- No submodule complexity

**Citation safety:** mech-interp-research repo (Dung's citation target) is
separate and untouched. how-models-think is local-only — nothing to break.
Will publish to GitHub after paper 2 is complete.
